About Me & My Mission

Hello! I'm Cezar Chiru, an embedded Linux engineer with 4 years of experience, from Romania, specializing in the Yocto Project. I've built custom distributions, created complex BSP layers, and optimized build times for large-scale projects.

I am offering free consultancy to help give back to the community and to gain more experience. Whether you're a hobbyist stuck on a build error or a professional looking for a second opinion, I'm here to help.

Services Offered

  • Recipe (.bb) and recipe append (.bbappend) debugging
  • Custom layer creation and review
  • Build configuration (local.conf, bblayers.conf) analysis
  • Understanding and fixing common BitBake errors
  • General advice on Yocto Project best practices

How It Works

This is a free, volunteer-based service. Here's the best way to get in touch:

  1. Open an Issue: Go to the "Issues" tab in this repository.
  2. Describe Your Problem: Create a new issue and describe your problem in as much detail as possible. Include error logs, your configuration, and what you've tried so far.
  3. I'll Respond: I will do my best to review your issue and provide feedback, suggestions, or a solution directly in the issue thread.
